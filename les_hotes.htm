Ikea crockery and furniture were chosen for the simplicity of their lines and their currency. Their shapes have been reproduced in weaving of adhesive tape, a transparent and flexible material that suggests the edges of the volumes. The furnitures imitated settles in the given space as if everything was normal. A sweet but obvious deceit spreads out and a light disorder invades us. We wonder about their reality, their temporality or our relationship to them.
